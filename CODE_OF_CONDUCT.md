# Code of Conduct

This project expects contributors and maintainers to interact professionally and respectfully.

## Expected Behavior

- Focus on technical issues and user impact
- Be direct without being hostile
- Assume good faith until there is evidence otherwise
- Respect differing levels of experience and background
- Accept constructive feedback and course correction

## Unacceptable Behavior

- Harassment, threats, or personal attacks
- Discrimination or hateful conduct
- Doxxing or sharing private information without consent
- Repeated bad-faith disruption, spam, or trolling
- Sexualized language or unwanted advances

## Scope

This code of conduct applies to repository spaces and project-related communication, including issues, pull requests, review threads, release discussions, and other project channels.

## Enforcement

Maintainers may edit, remove, or moderate content that violates these rules and may temporarily or permanently restrict participation when necessary to keep the project healthy.

## Reporting

Report conduct problems to the maintainers through a private channel when possible. Avoid turning personal reports into public issue threads.
